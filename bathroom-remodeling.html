Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a bathroom remodel cost in Dallas?

Costs vary widely based on size and materials, but a guest bathroom remodel typically starts around $15k, while a primary master bathroom can range from $30k to $75k+.

What's the difference between a wet room and a standard bathroom?

A wet room is a fully waterproofed bathroom, typically with an open, curbless shower. This creates a very modern, accessible, and spacious feel.

How long does a bathroom remodel take?

A standard bathroom remodel usually takes 3 to 5 weeks, depending on the complexity and scope of the project.
