Frequently Asked Questions

Should we move out during a whole home renovation?

For most large-scale renovations, we highly recommend arranging for alternative living accommodations. This allows our crews to work more efficiently and safely, and it is far more comfortable for your family.

How do you keep a large project on budget?

We use detailed, line-item estimates and a transparent change-order process. All selections are made and priced before construction begins to minimize surprises. Clear communication is key.

How long does a whole home renovation take?

This depends entirely on the scope, but projects can range from 3 to 9 months or more. We provide a detailed timeline before any work begins.
