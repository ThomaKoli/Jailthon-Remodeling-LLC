Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical Dallas kitchen remodel take?

A typical kitchen remodel can take anywhere from 4 to 8 weeks, depending on the scope of work, material availability, and inspections.

What are the most popular countertop materials right now?

Quartz remains the most popular choice for its durability, low maintenance, and wide range of styles. Granite and quartzite are also excellent, high-end options.

Do I need a permit for my kitchen remodel in Dallas?

Yes, most kitchen remodels that involve moving plumbing, electrical, or walls will require a permit from the City of Dallas. We handle the entire permitting process for you.

How do you manage dust and debris during the project?

We take great care to protect your home. We seal off the work area with plastic barriers, use dust extractors on our tools, and clean the site daily.
