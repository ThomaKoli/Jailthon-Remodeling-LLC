Frequently Asked Questions

What is LVP and why is it so popular?

Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP) is a highly durable, waterproof flooring material that realistically mimics the look of wood or stone. It's popular for its resilience, ease of maintenance, and cost-effectiveness, making it ideal for families and pet owners.

What size tile is best for a small bathroom?

Contrary to popular belief, using large-format tiles (e.g., 12x24 or 24x24) in a small bathroom can actually make it feel larger because there are fewer grout lines to break up the space.

Do you help with material selection?

Absolutely. We can provide samples and guide you to the best materials for your project's style, budget, and functional needs.
