Case Study: Preston Hollow Kitchen Transformation

From dark and dated to bright and beautiful.

The Story

The Challenge:

The client's kitchen was dark, dated, and lacked functional storage for their growing family. A poorly placed peninsula cut off the flow, making the space feel cramped and inefficient for entertaining.

The Solution:

We opened up the space by removing a non-load-bearing wall, which instantly created a better connection to the living area. We designed a large central island with ample prep space and seating. Custom shaker-style cabinetry with smart storage solutions, like pull-out pantries and deep drawers, maximized every inch of space.

The Result:

A bright, open, and highly functional kitchen that has become the family's favorite gathering spot. The combination of Calacatta quartz, warm oak flooring, and modern gold fixtures creates a timeless yet contemporary feel that dramatically increased the home's value and the family's enjoyment of it.
